HyperBet Protocol

HyperBet is a decentralized sports betting protocol deployed on HyperEVM. It combines prediction markets, traditional sportsbook mechanics, and DeFi liquidity vaults into a unified protocol.

Key Features

  • Institutional-grade risk engine with Kelly-criterion position sizing
  • Decentralized oracle architecture with anti-manipulation safeguards
  • Hyper Liquidity Pool (HLP) — earn yield by being the house
  • Cross-market exposure accounting for correlated events
  • UUPS upgradeable smart contract architecture

How It Works

The protocol operates a Hyper Liquidity Pool (HLP) that acts as the counterparty to all bets. Liquidity providers deposit USDC into the HLP and earn yield from the house edge.

Bet Lifecycle

1User selects event + outcome + stake
2Risk Engine validates bet (exposure, Kelly, solvency)
3Liquidity locked from HLP for potential payout
4Oracle resolves event outcome
5Winner claims payout / Loser's stake distributed

Placing Bets

Select any active market, choose your outcome, and enter your stake. The protocol automatically adjusts odds based on current pool exposure and utilization.

FAQ

What chain is HyperBet on?

HyperEVM — a high-performance EVM-compatible chain.

What currency do I bet with?

USDC (6 decimals). All bets and vault deposits are in USDC.

How are odds determined?

Base odds come from an oracle aggregator, then adjusted by the risk engine based on pool utilization, event exposure, and correlation factors.

Can I lose more than my stake?

No. Your maximum loss is always limited to your stake amount.
